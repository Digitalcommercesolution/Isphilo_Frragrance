Our Essence & Story

"A scent is more than just a fragrance; it's an extension of your personality."

ISIPHILO FRAGRANCES was established on 29 March 2018 in the heart of Berea, Durban (4083), with a singular vision: to bring the luxury of premium fragrances to everyone. We believe that every individual deserves to experience the confidence and elegance that comes with a signature scent.

Our journey began with a passion for exquisite aromas and a commitment to quality. Rooted in the vibrant culture of KwaZulu-Natal, we have meticulously curated a collection of unique blends that resonate with both local heritage and global sophistication.

Today, while we remain proud of our Berea roots, ISIPHILO FRAGRANCES has expanded its reach to serve customers worldwide. Every bottle tells a story of craftsmanship, dedication, and the pursuit of olfactory perfection, delivered from South Africa to your doorstep.
